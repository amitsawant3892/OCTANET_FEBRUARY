About us..!

DY Patil Institute of Technology, Pimpri, Pune was established in 1998. It offers UG and PG courses like BE, ME, MBA, and PhD. The College is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and DTE Maharashtra and is recognised by NBA. The ISO 9001:2008 certified college received ‘A’ Grade accreditation from the NAAC. Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, it has well-equipped laboratories with ultra-modern facilities supported by highly qualified teaching faculty in all branches. The Central Library is furnished with reference books, textbooks, national and international journals and an internet facility as a part of informal teaching. The College is a consortium member of Indo-US Collaboration in Engineering Education (IUCEE) and the International Federation for Engineering Education (IFEES).
